meet your twin

twin.md is a desk creature that mirrors your state. it reads your claude sessions and your second brain, and occasionally tells you to take a break. everything stays on your machine.

your axolotl twin

default art is a cute chibi axolotl. you can also describe a custom look for evolution.

~/.claude/ directory

this is where twin.md reads your claude sessions and writes its own state. it also stores your ai provider settings here.

obsidian vault (optional)

twin reads daily notes, tags, and unfinished todos from your vault to figure out what you're thinking about. nothing leaves your disk.

ai provider

an API key is required for chat, sprite evolution, and brain-aware replies. the key is stored on disk under ~/.claude/twin-ai.json (permissions 600) by default.
