meet your twin

twin.md is a desk creature that mirrors your state. it reads your claude sessions and your second brain, and occasionally tells you to take a break. everything stays on your machine.

you & your vault

your name, and optionally where to read your obsidian notes.

~/.claude/ directory

this is where twin.md reads your claude sessions and writes its own state. it also stores your ai provider settings here.

how should your twin look?

default is the classic chibi axolotl. custom = you describe a creature; you'll preview it after the api key step.

ai provider

an API key is required for chat, sprite evolution, and brain-aware replies. stored in ~/.claude/twin-ai.json (permissions 600) by default.

sprites & /image (openai & google) use rembg for background removal. install once on your machine: pipx install "rembg[cpu,cli]"

only sent to the provider you pick. you can add rembg in parallel; it's not required to finish this screen.
